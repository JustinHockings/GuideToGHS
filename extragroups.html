Guide to Glenwood High School

Extra Groups

There are numerous activities at Glenwood High School that new students can get themselves involved in. These range from social groups to clubs that are more skill oriented, even to clubs that provide connections with like minded people. Some of these groups include:

  • Tabletop gaming club
  • Ballet company
  • Chess club
  • Duke of Edinburgh
  • Chill chat and colour
  • Glenwood High School band

There are also a wide variety of both grade and recreational sports that you can join. Some of these sports are:

  • Bowling
  • Rock climbing
  • Table tennis
  • Grade Sport girls touch football
  • Grade Sport boys soccer
  • Grade Sport mixed basketball

These activities are all available throughout different times of the school week for the entirety of the school year, and will serve as a great way for students to acquaint themselves with the school itself, and meet different students with similar interests.
