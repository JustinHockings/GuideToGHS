Guide to Glenwood High School

Facilities

Glenwood High School provides many additional facilities outside traditional learning environments. Some of these places include:

  • Basketball Court - Open at recess and lunch, and can be used in PE practical classes and during Wednesday sport.
  • Library - Open at recess and lunch, with the exception of Friday recesses.
  • Canteen - Open at recess and lunch.
  • Learning Center - Can be accessed any time.
  • Staffrooms - Can be accessed by students at lunch times only.
  • Gym - Available to PE classes.
  • Bathrooms - Available at recess and lunch. The office bathroom can be used in class time if you sign in.

It's important to note that with staffrooms, there are specific locations for each faculty. For example, the PE and Maths staffrooms are located on the left side of P block. Alternatively, the rest of the faculties can be found in F block, divided into the East and West staffrooms.

Staffroom East

  • English Faculty
  • Science Faculty
  • Languages Faculty

Staffroom West

  • HSIE Faculty
  • CAPA Faculty
  • Industrial Arts Faculty
  • Home Economics Faculty
