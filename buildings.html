Guide to Glenwood High School

Buildings and Classrooms

A new challenge every time anyone is in a new school is figuring out all of the new blocks and buildings, and where they are located. In Glenwood High School there are 12 blocks, labelled the following: A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I, J, K, and P. Not all of these blocks contain classrooms, but they all are part of the Glenwood High School grounds.

This list below has been condensed to highlight only blocks that frequently have classes in them.

  • A Block - Has 6 classrooms with one being a computer room. The block has a general focus on HSIE subjects, and the bottom of the block contains the learning hub.
  • B Block - Has 12 classrooms spread over a top and bottom level. It has no specific subjects and has a single computer room.
  • C Block - Has a top level which is focused on PE and PASS subjects, whilst the bottom of C block has two music rooms and a music practical room.
  • D Block - Has only a top level and mainly focuses on the subject of English.
  • E Block - Has 4 classrooms that only focus on Science as a subject, and has two practical rooms with equipment for scientific experiments.
  • G Block - Has 4 classrooms focused on Home Economics, and two practical kitchens at the back for Food Technology and Hospitality subjects.
  • H Block - Has 4 classrooms, including a computer room, focused on Industrial Technology subjects. Two materials workshops are located at the back for Wood/Metal work.
  • J Block - Has 4 classrooms for CAPA subjects, including an art practical room and a dance studio. Occasionally some English classes may be held here.
  • P Block - Has 19 classrooms total, having 13 for general learning with a focus on maths. There are a few specialist rooms for art, science, woodwork, and cooking too.
